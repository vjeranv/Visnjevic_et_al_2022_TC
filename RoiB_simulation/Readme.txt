Here are the Sif files and corresponding .f90 procedures used to simulate the Age field of Roi Baudouin ice shelf, East antarctica_ice_velocity_450m_v2
Data used and its sources are detailed in the manuscript. Age solver used is from Martin and Gudmundsson (2012).

Code should be run:
1) ini1.sif
2) ini2.sif
3) RoiB_run.sif
